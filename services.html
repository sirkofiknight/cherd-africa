Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for health equity and research excellence

CHERD Africa offers full project management services in health and allied sciences, providing specialized expertise across multiple domains to ensure evidence-based solutions and sustainable impact.

Core Service Areas

Project Management & Research Implementation

Expert management of health research projects from inception to completion.

We provide comprehensive project management services ensuring all research is conducted in accordance with local and international ethical standards. Our team prepares scientific reports, manages procurement, organizes field operations, oversees budget management, and conducts financial audits.

  • Scientific report preparation
  • Ethical compliance monitoring
  • Procurement management
  • Field organization and logistics
  • Budget oversight and financial audits

Data Management

Modern data systems for secure and efficient health information management.

CHERD Africa recognizes that modern data management systems are critical for effective healthcare delivery. We utilize cutting-edge technologies including Oracle, InForm, REDCap, and MySQL to provide secure electronic data storage and management, replacing outdated paper-based record systems.

  • Electronic data capture systems
  • Database design and implementation
  • Data security and compliance
  • Oracle, InForm, REDCap, MySQL expertise
  • Data migration and integration
Secure Storage

Grant Writing

Professional grant development services to secure funding for impactful projects.

Our grant writing team conducts comprehensive research to identify the most relevant funding opportunities and develops customized proposals that compellingly communicate your project's impact. We provide end-to-end support from proposal development to post-award compliance.

  • Post-award compliance assistance

Data Analysis

Advanced statistical analysis for evidence-based decision making.

Our expert team employs SPSS, Stata, and R software for comprehensive data analysis. We provide data cleaning, validation, integrity checking, and specialized analyses including logistic regression, survival analysis, and geospatial mapping to derive meaningful insights from research data.

  • SPSS, Stata, and R expertise
  • Data cleaning and validation
  • Statistical modeling
  • Geospatial mapping and GIS
  • Predictive analytics
