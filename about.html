About CHERD Africa

Centre for Health Equity Research and Development in Africa

Our Mission

To collaborate with partners ensuring evidence-based research and analytics forms the basis for policy and practice to alleviate suffering, health inequalities and improved social inclusion among the African population.

Our Vision

Achieving health equity for diverse population in rural and urban settings in Africa through sustainable, evidence-based interventions and collaborative partnerships.

Our Purpose

Adopting a data-driven approach to build scientific and sustainable solutions that create health equity for all, ensuring no one is left behind.

Who We Are

CHERD Africa is a Ghanaian Non-Governmental Organization that offers full project management services in health and allied sciences as well as specialized expertise in a number of key research areas.

We provide tailor-made solutions grounded in information and technology for expert research management. Our organization is committed to ensuring that evidence-based research and analytics form the basis for policy and practice across Africa.

With a multidisciplinary team of experts, we combine local knowledge with international best practices to deliver sustainable health solutions that make a real difference in communities across the continent.
