Who We Are

Centre for Health Equity Research and Development in Africa

CHERD Africa is a Ghanaian NGO offering comprehensive project management services in health and allied sciences. We provide specialized expertise in research areas, ensuring evidence-based solutions that create health equity for all.

Our Mission

To collaborate with partners ensuring evidence-based research and analytics forms the basis for policy and practice to reduce suffering and health inequalities among African populations.

Our Vision

Achieving health equity for diverse populations in rural and urban settings in Africa.
